Introduction

The ITI AI Playground supports students, educators, employees, and administrators with responsible AI enablement backed by governance controls and operational workflows.

Objectives & Strategic Goals

  • Enable controlled access to AI services.
  • Ensure policy compliance and ethical usage.
  • Equip educators with reusable prompt assets.
  • Enhance visibility, transparency, and accountability.

Scope of the System

  • Service catalog management and ownership.
  • End-to-end workflows for request approvals.
  • Policy publication, versioning, and enforcement.
  • Prompt Library lifecycle management.
